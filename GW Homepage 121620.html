About the Artist

Fenrir Fangthorn

Bad ass artist who loves to draw Modern Mercenaries, along with Middle Age Fantasy characters. Interested in DnD and drawing characters from it. Not only does he have a thing for art he also loves to make stories of his characters. He was worked on these for many years and has been devoted to creating vast worlds in them. Recently, he has discovered DnD and hope to build up his skills from that.

In his spare time he is a tough as nails bandit highwayman who robs from the greedy and corrupt king and his followers. Using his skills as a Gloom Ranger, he terrorises those who abuse their power and gives back to the poor and powerless. He is reveared by the peasants as a folk hero, vilified by the royalty and noblemen as nothing more than a ruthless criminal.
